Annika Viswesh

Hello, my name is Annika. I am a senior at Caltech majoring in Computer Science.

I am fortunate to have been mentored by Prof. Frances Arnold, Prof. Yisong Yue, Prof. Adam Wierman, and Jason Yang from Caltech.

My core interest is in developing multi-scale, interpretable machine learning models to uncover the mechanisms governing complex biological processes, from molecular interactions to pathway-level dynamics.

My approach has drawn on geometric deep learning to represent 3D molecular structures, generative modeling to simulate gene expression responses to perturbations, and causal framing to explain pathway-level responses.

I am currently working with Dr. Chandler Squires, Prof. Yisong Yue, and Prof. Pradeep Ravikumar. Prior to Caltech, I have also worked with Prof. Soichi Wakasuki at Stanford. I am forever grateful to have had the opportunity to learn and collaborate with truly amazing people on various projects.

I am always eager to discuss ideas and welcome any questions or comments about my research.
